Hobo Ramen

Description

Modify Maruchan ramen so that it satisfies all your hobo desires!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of Maruchan Ramen
  • Bag of sliced carrots
  • Hard boiled eggs
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Pepporoni slices
  • Sriracha
  • Cucumbers
  • Shredded cheese
  • Colorful peppers

Steps

  1. Follow the directions on the Maruchan Ramn bag.
  2. Chop the peppers, cucumbers, tomatoes and eggs and add them to the ramen bowl.
  3. Add the cooked ramen to the bowl.
  4. Add sriracha to the ramen.
